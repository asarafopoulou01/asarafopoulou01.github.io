Can I have more than one uplift for the same product?

ODBMS can only hold one uplift per product/per PO.
However once Imports cost the shipment, all margins are automatically adjusted.
However, you can either raise a new PO to have two separate transport methos so the margins are more accurate.
Imports aim to have all shipments costed within 3 days of delivery so that margins are corrected at the earliest possibility, ideally this would be before any sales are made.

What is SADC? Who pays for what?

SADC means sea air difference. RI pay for the sea freight and the supplier pay for the Air.

How to find a net landed cost?

To find a net landed cost - Go to the tab 'Costs ' then click on ' Product Cost Enquiry'. Type in the product you need the landed cost for then ' Get Detail'. You will see the 'Landed cost' in the 'Landed Cost' Box.
